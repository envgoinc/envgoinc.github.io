## Colors
- **Background**: Cruise Blue `#121D7A` — `233 74% 27%` (used as dark base)
- **Marine Blue**: `#28C4FC` — `191 84% 53%` — primary accent, eyebrows, CTAs, hover states, dividers
- **Neon Turq**: `#16E0FA` — `185 91% 54%` — secondary accent, gradients
- **Fireside**: `#FF4501` — `16 100% 50%` — sparingly for emphasis, large text/UI only
- **Snow**: `#FFFFFF` — headings, primary text
- **Sky Blue**: `#D1EEFA` — `200 77% 90%` — subtle backgrounds, light accents
- **Grey**: `#A0B4C8` — `205 30% 72%` — body text, descriptions
- **Card Background**: `hsl(230 74% 14%)`
- **Section Alt**: `hsl(230 50% 12%)`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Manrope (Google Fonts) — light to medium weight
- **Body**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Subheadings**: Inter (Google Fonts)

## Rules
- Marine Blue accent used sparingly: eyebrows, stat numbers, dividers, hover states, CTAs
- Fireside orange: large text/UI accents only, never small text
- No decorative elements. Clean geometric layout.
- Dark-to-darker section transitions only
- All section max-widths: 1100px centred
- Min section vertical padding: 100px
- Image placeholders with descriptive labels until final assets
